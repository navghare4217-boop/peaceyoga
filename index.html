About Us

Traditional roots, a modern approach.

We are committed to providing a holistic yoga program for individuals, groups and companies — everywhere. What sets us apart is a yoga curriculum rooted in the tradition of classical yoga, combined with a modern, scientific approach.

Our classes offer an authentic experience composed of pranayama (breathwork), asana (posture) and dhyana (meditation), delivered across a range of yoga themes, with proper skill and technique at the centre of every session.

Meet the Founder

Master Krushna Tonde

Founder of Peace Yoga Academy and a highly accomplished Yoga Master with over 15 years of dedicated experience in teaching and promoting authentic yoga. His mission is to inspire individuals toward physical health, mental well-being and holistic living, through traditional yogic practice combined with a scientific approach.

He is an Authorized National Yogasana Coach certified by Yogasanabharat and NS NIS Patiala, where he secured First Rank among 140 national yoga coaches. He also serves as the Indian Ambassador of Yogasana to Europe and is an International Authorized Yogasana Coach, actively promoting Yogasana on global platforms.

Beyond yoga, he holds a 1st DAN Black Belt in Taekwondo — a reflection of the discipline and physical fitness he brings to his teaching. Through his philosophy of tradition meeting modern science, he has trained more than 2,500 students, helping them build strength, flexibility, confidence and lifelong well-being.

Academic & Professional Qualifications

  • Master of Arts (M.A.) in Yoga (Yogashastra)
  • International Certified Advanced Yoga Teacher
  • RYT 500 Certified by Yoga Alliance USA
  • Authorised National & World Coach, Yogasanabharat

Major Achievements

A decade of milestones, on and off the mat.

2023

Honoured with the prestigious Marathwada Bhushan Award during the 75th Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, in recognition of his outstanding contribution to the field of yoga.

2024

Trained 150 national yoga coaches at NS NIS Patiala — the Sports Authority of India, Punjab.

2025

Played a significant role in creating yoga history, contributing to 12 Guinness World Records achieved in Bengaluru.

2026

Served as an International Authorized Yogasana Coach for participants from Armenia and the Netherlands at the 1st World Yogasana Championship, held in Ahmedabad.

Why Peace Yoga Academy

Planned, systematic, complete.

70 / 30

Global

A course designed to let you teach both locally in India and globally, backed by Yoga Alliance USA certification.

Anatomy

Anatomy and physiology, often difficult to grasp, are taught in a simple and effective way by teachers who live classical yoga daily.

Curriculum

What the 200-hour course covers.

Anatomy and physiology — theory and practice
Pranayama and Shatkarma (cleansing techniques)
Advanced asana classes — backbending, hip opening, arm balancing
Sun salutation and its variations
Common traditional asana alignment
Teaching methodology — class design, sequencing, preparation
Class themes — Hatha, Beginner, Ananda, Body Opening and more
Teaching with props — block, strap, wall
Classroom skills and building class topics
Alignment skills for members of all levels
